emilio molina

I am a Research Engineer at BMAT Licensing S.L., in the R&D team. My main job is to explore new approaches in order to improve our technology, and to implement them into production-ready code. I typically do research and data science in python (jupyter, keras, tensorflow, matplotlib, scikit-learn, pandas, django, essentia, librosa, etc.), and I develop production code mainly in python and C++.

Regarding my background, I have a PhD by University of Málaga in the field of Music Information Retrieval, a MSc degree in sound and music computing at the Music Technology Group of the Universitat Pompeu Fabra (Barcelona), and a degree in telecommunications engineering (audio and image specialty) in University of Málaga.
